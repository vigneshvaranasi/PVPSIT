About

Discover the Literary Club of CSE Department a vibrant community celebrating the art of words and self-expression. Whether you're an aspiring writer or a devoted reader, our club offers a platform to explore literature's diverse dimensions.

Here's what we offer:

  • Workshops
  • Book Talks
  • Open Mic
  • Guest Speakers
  • Competitions

Join our Literary Club for an enriching journey of words, ideas, and connections. Whether you seek to refine your writing, engage in insightful conversations, or simply revel in the literary realm, come be a part of our expressive haven. Let's celebrate words together!
