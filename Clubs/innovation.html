About

Welcome to the College Innovation Club, a thriving center for creativity, exploration, and collaboration! We are delighted to welcome you to our community of aspiring innovators and change makers.

We believe in providing a platform for the development of innovative mindsets and entrepreneurship training. Your ideas will blossom here, as well as the skills needed to turn them into impactful projects and ventures.

What We Do:

  1. Workshops and Training
  2. Idea Incubation
  3. Hackathons and Challenges
  4. Guest Lectures and Talks
  5. Networking and Community
  6. Interdisciplinary Project Exhibitions
